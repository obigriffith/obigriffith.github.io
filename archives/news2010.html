10 Oct 2010 - Well hello. Probably nobody ever checks this blog since I so rarely post. But, here's an update on what I've been up to anyways. I'm pretty much settled into Berkeley now. I lived in a temporary place for a few months. It had a great yard like a mini botanical garden. Tinkering around there was a nice creative outlet for me. However, recently I have found myself a more permanent home. It has a nice yard too. I've also been spending way too much time in my second home, the office (like right now)! But, I have found time to explore Berkeley (Hills, Marcia's Garden, Tilden, UC Berkeley botanical garden) and take day trips to SF (Golden Gate park). I had a great visit from Shirley this summer and we went to many great places like Pinnacles, Napa and Meritage Resort, Stinson Beach, Point Reyes, and all over SF and Oakland (Flickr collection of all sets). I've already been on trips to Washington DC, Dallas and Houston for conferences/meetings. And of course I was back in Winnipeg to see my little brother married! I bought a new Subaru Impreza so I'm definitely ready for skiing at Tahoe this winter. :-) My twin got himself a fancy new camera and has started collecting some sweet lenses and filters. He's taking some really cool pictures and finally has his own flickr page. He's also getting more into motorcycle riding. I'm jealous on both fronts and have no intention of being left behind. ;-) In the music department I've been to a few good concerts including Neil Young at the Fox Theater in Oakland, Pantha Du Prince at the Independant, the Hardly Strictly Blue Grass Festival in Golden Gate Park, and Arcade Fire at the Greek Theater on UC Berkeley Campus (that one was awesome almost beyond words). Next weekend I'm going to the Treasure Island Music Festival to see an awesome line-up of Belle and Sebastien, The National, Rogue Wave, Ra Ra Riot and more. I'm psyched for that and listening to their latest stuff to get me up to speed. I'll try to post some more news about how it goes. But, for now, back to work! I'm writing a review on genomic methods for detecting and predicting breast cancer metastasis. Ciao.

5 Mar 2010 - I'm now in Berkeley and just finished moving this website over to its new home. This shouldn't affect you in any way. So much has happened since my last post that it seems almost ridiculous to try and list it all here. The short version is that I went travelling in Europe (Flickr) and Roatan (Flickr). Then, I started a new position at Lawrence Berkeley Lab in Berkeley CA. I'm getting up to speed, learning a lot about breast cancer, and starting to narrow in on a project. More news and pictures to follow.

13 Jan 2009 - This will be a short post because I'm connecting by ssh on a slow wireless connection from my hotel in Marseille. Not to mention, using the brutal mac OS terminal app and emacs to modify the html for this webpage directly. Used to do things this way all the time. But, now it seems so passe. See I'm learning some french. haha. Anyways, just wanted to say that I'm alive and well, having a great vacation in Europe. Here's a link to my Flickr collection for the trip in case you want to follow along with the photos. So far, I've had suprisingly good luck finding internet access and uploading photos almost daily. Can't promise this will continue in Greece or Honduras as we continue our search for warmer weather. :)
